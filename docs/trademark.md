# ⚠️ Trademark Notice — "Qualabinance"

> **Read before using this name publicly or commercially.**

## 🎯 Background

The name **"Qualabinance"** is a portmanteau of:

- **Qual** — short for *Quantitative*
- **la** — short for *lab* / *alpha*
- **binance** — the cryptocurrency exchange

This was a creative choice by the founder (CEO of `open-uppu`) to evoke the project's dual nature: a **quant laboratory** with strong **crypto focus**.

---

## ⚠️ Trademark Risk

**"BINANCE"** is a registered trademark of **Binance Holdings Ltd.** (and related entities).

Using "Qualabinance" in certain contexts may raise trademark concerns, including:
- Risk of consumer confusion
- Dilution of the Binance brand
- Implied endorsement or affiliation

---

## ✅ Acceptable uses

The name is **safe to use** in the following contexts:
- ✅ **Personal projects** — R&D, learning, experimentation
- ✅ **Open-source / non-commercial** — public GitHub repo, research papers
- ✅ **Internal documents** — within the open-uppu organization
- ✅ **Academic / educational** — university coursework, conference talks

---

## ⚠️ Risky uses

The following uses **may require legal review**:
- ⚠️ **Commercial product** — paid SaaS, subscription service
- ⚠️ **Marketing** — paid ads, promotional materials
- ⚠️ **Domain names** — `qualabinance.com`, `qualabinance.io`, `qualabinance.ai`
- ⚠️ **Trademark filings** — would likely be refused

---

## 🔄 If rebrand is needed

If the project becomes commercial or faces legal pushback, **rename options**:

| Name | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| `qualafinance` | Same vibe, no Binance wordmark | Generic-sounding |
| `qualab` | Short, unique | Loses "finance" hint |
| `alphacore` | Clean, no trademark | Loses crypto angle |
| `qualance` | Distinctive | Inverts to sounds like "violence" |

**Recommended**: `qualafinance` for commercial, `qualabinance` for personal.
